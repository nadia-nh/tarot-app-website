Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Arcana Flux mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information handled by the app.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Arcana Flux does not collect, transmit, or share any personal identification information (PII).

  • Local Data: Your tarot reading history and favorite cards are stored exclusively on your device using an internal database (Room). We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.
  • Network Data: The app uses your internet connection to fetch tarot card descriptions and images from our API. These requests are anonymous and do not contain user-specific identifiers.

2. Third-Party Services

While the app itself does not collect data, it is distributed via the Google Play Store, which may collect information as described in their own privacy policies (e.g., crash reports or basic device analytics).

3. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to function correctly:

  • INTERNET: Required to fetch card data and updates.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check if your device is online to provide a better user experience.

4. Children's Privacy

Arcana Flux does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
